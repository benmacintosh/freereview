The Free Review of Propositions

in the past month I rebuilt a street near my house, in my head

most days I cycled past slowly. I met it's family of turned posts, front doors, architraves, bargeboards, window aprons, flyscreens..

when I talk to you on the phone, let's check that we are facing each other, so if we walk straight we would meet

where does this street face?

let's fold my street straight through to yours:

along the way, building parts stop when they meet and fit with another place perfectly

let's keeping going past our streets and fold through the world

all the skyscrapers are together happily, so their windows can be doors between them

little cottages sit inside mansions

plains of freeways lie side by side

a confederacy of brick verandahs and weatherboard walls back to back

office foyers are brightly packed with street lights..

we just have to pick the best line to fold through

and keep in mind what might be left over
